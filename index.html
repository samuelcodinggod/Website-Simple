Privacy Policy for Allyra

Last updated: October 2025

Introduction

Allyra ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Quiz Responses: Your answers to career assessment questions to generate personalized recommendations
  • Selfie Photos: Optional photos you choose to upload for AI-generated career images
  • Career History: Your saved career recommendations and quiz results

Device Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage analytics and app performance data
  • Crash reports and error logs

Payment Information

  • Purchase history and subscription status (processed securely through Apple's App Store)
  • No credit card information is stored by our app

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Generate personalized career recommendations based on your quiz responses
  • Create AI-generated images using your selfie photos (if provided)
  • Save and display your career history and recommendations
  • Provide access to premium features after payment

App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance
  • Develop new features and content

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our app functionality:

OpenAI (GPT API)

  • Processes your quiz responses to generate career recommendations
  • Data shared: Quiz answers and responses
  • Purpose: AI-powered career matching
  • Privacy: OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this data processing

Google Gemini

  • Generates AI images based on your selfie photos and career recommendations
  • Data shared: Selfie photos and career titles
  • Purpose: AI image generation
  • Privacy: Google's privacy policy applies to this data processing

Superwall

  • Manages paywall functionality and subscription features
  • Data shared: App usage data and subscription status
  • Purpose: Payment processing and feature access control
  • Privacy: Superwall's privacy policy applies to this data processing

Apple App Store

  • Processes payments and manages subscriptions
  • Data shared: Purchase history and subscription status
  • Purpose: Payment processing and app distribution
  • Privacy: Apple's privacy policy applies to this data processing

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Quiz responses and career history are stored locally on your device
  • Selfie photos are stored locally and can be deleted at any time
  • Payment status is stored locally for app functionality

Cloud Storage

  • No personal data is stored in our own cloud servers
  • Data is only processed by third-party AI services as described above
  • Third-party services may temporarily store data for processing purposes

Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • API keys and sensitive information are stored securely
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • View your saved career recommendations in the app
  • Delete your selfie photos at any time
  • Clear your quiz history and start over
  • Manage your subscription through Apple's App Store settings

Data Deletion

  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • Third-party services may retain data according to their own policies
  • Contact us to request additional data deletion assistance

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
